Decoupling — User Service aur Mail Service ek doosre ko directly call nahi karte, RabbitMQ beech mein hai
Microservices — dono services independently kaam karti hain, ek band ho toh doosri chalta rehta hai
RabbitMQ ka fayda — agar Mail Service momentarily down ho, message queue mein safe pada rahega aur jab service wapas aayegi toh process ho jaega — message lost nahi hoga
